# Origin

AI Vendor Concentration Watch exists because executive teams often know they are becoming too dependent on one AI supplier before they can point to the exact lane where lock-in, pricing power, compliance dependency, or switching friction is accumulating. Kinetic Gain needs one reusable surface that shows where concentration is rising, where fallback readiness is weak, where contracts are gaining too much leverage, and what should be diversified before the next board or investor review.
